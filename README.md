## 🚀 What I’m focused on now

### **NelCapeTown (Pty) Ltd**
My company is the home of my consulting, product development, and educational work — where software engineering meets design thinking, AI, and a healthy respect for clean architecture.

### **LLM Lagoon**
I’m building **LLM Lagoon**, a developer-first platform for capturing, indexing, structuring, and exploring LLM conversations and outputs across tools and workflows.

It is designed for technically sophisticated users who want:
- better prompt and response traceability
- structured storage and retrieval
- workflow automation
- local-first control over AI-generated knowledge

This project sits right at the intersection of:  
**LLMs, embeddings, automation, developer tooling, and knowledge architecture**.

### **Nuxeo Digital Asset / Document Management Course**
I’m also developing a **course and curriculum around Nuxeo-based digital asset and document management systems**, with supporting work around:
- metadata
- search
- AI-enhanced information workflows
- containerised deployment
- modern enterprise content architecture

The plan is to host this educational content via **Moodle**.

### **Alignerr / Labelbox**
I’m also working with **Alignerr / Labelbox** as I continue expanding my hands-on involvement in AI-related work, drawing on my software background, technical judgement, and long-standing interest in machine learning and intelligent systems.
